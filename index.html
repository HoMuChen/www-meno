About Meno

Revolutionizing how you capture and organize meeting information

Turning Meetings into Actionable Insights

Meno specializes in transforming your meeting audio into structured, actionable documentation. Whether you're recording live meetings or uploading audio files, our platform generates comprehensive summaries that capture key decisions, conclusions, and action items.

  • Audio transcription via recording or file upload
  • Automated summary generation
  • Meeting decisions and conclusions extraction
  • Requirements lists and to-do items identification
  • Future: Project management integration
  • Future: Client management with speaker identification
